The Best Movies According to Alecks

My top 3 movies as of 2025

Bullet Train Explosion

It is a Japanese movie wherein the plot twist is quite predictable near at the end but still a good movie to watch.

KPop Demon Hunter

It is an animated movie inspired by the Kpop culture. The story follows a group of Kpop idols who secretly fight demons in their spare time.

Otto

It is a heartwarming story about a grumpy yet lovable man who finds purpose in his life through an unexpected friendship with a stray dog.
